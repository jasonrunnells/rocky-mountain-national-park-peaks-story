Olympic National Park

A recreation of the official Olympic National Park map designed to accurately replicate National Park Service styles, symbology, color, and layout.

Jason Runnells | March, 2026

Overview

This map presents Olympic National Park through a detailed recreation of its official cartographic style, combining terrain visualization with recreational and geographic features. Shaded relief and subtle color variation are used to define elevation and landscape patterns, while roads, trails, and key locations are organized through a clear visual hierarchy. The design prioritizes balance and readability, resulting in a map that is both informative and visually cohesive.
